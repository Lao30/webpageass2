Contribute As a Volunteer

Bamboo School is always in need of passionate volunteers to help bring hope, education, and joy to street children in Bali. By volunteering here, you’re not just teaching you’re creating a safe space where children feel seen, supported, and inspired to dream bigger. Whether you teach reading, art, science, or simply bring kindness, your presence can make a lasting impact. Join us and be part of a meaningful journey that changes lives both theirs and yours.

Want to Donate?

Your support helps us provide education, food, school supplies, and a safe space for street children in Bali. Every donation, big or small, makes a real difference. Join us in creating brighter futures one child at a time.
