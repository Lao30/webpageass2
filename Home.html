Together we build a stronger community through education and compassion.

Our Mission

Giving disadvantaged children here in Bali the loving care, education, and skills they need to grow.

Higlight Programs

Kejar Paket Class

A flexible learning program that helps children complete their basic education (Package A, B, or C) in a supportive and child-friendly environment.

Volunteer Class

In Volunteer Class, children learn new and exciting things from visiting volunteers. The topics vary depending on each volunteer’s skills ranging from arts and languages to life skills and fun activities. Every session is a chance to learn, grow, and be inspired!

Smiling Trip Project

An educational and fun outing program for children. Sometimes we visit exciting places like the zoo, the beach, or a volunteer’s home. Through these trips, children can play, learn new things, and create joyful memories with friends and volunteers.

Call for Volunteer

We're looking for passionate individuals who want to make a real difference in the community. Be part of our volunteer team and help bring meaningful impact through our social initiatives. Your time matters!
